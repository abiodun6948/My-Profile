Volunteering in Conakry-Africa!!
  • 31 May 2020
  • 0

A 6 Days Training organized by USAID-WINROCK INTERNATIONAL (Farmer-to-Farmer Program) on GUI296 –Solar Energy Training for NGO Solidarité, Dalaba, Guinea Conakry facilitated By Dr. Abiodun E. Abioye(Volunteer) I had the incredible opportunity to facilitate a 6-day Solar Energy Training organized by USAID-Winrock International (Farmer-to-Farmer Program) for NGO Solidarité in Dalaba, Guinea Conakry. From June 5th to 11th, 2024, over 40 participants, including farmers, market women, and students, learned how to design and construct solar cookers, dryers, ovens, and solar-powered electric cookers.
